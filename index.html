Commercial Clients

Managing the landscape of a commercial property shouldn't be a headache. Whether you own an apartment complex, oversee an office park, manage a retail center, or run an HOA — we step in as your dedicated landscape consultant. From on-site visits and hands-on property assessments to reviewing your contractor agreements and making sure the work gets done right, we handle the details so you don't have to. Think of us as an extension of your management team, focused entirely on keeping your grounds looking their best year-round.

What We Bring to the Table:

  • On-site property visits & landscape audits
  • Landscape contractor & vendor contract review
  • Full landscape management oversight
  • Seasonal maintenance planning & scheduling
  • Plant selection, design & material specifications
  • Vendor evaluation, bidding & contractor oversight
  • Pest, disease & turf management consulting
  • Ongoing advisory & monthly check-ins

Meet Matt

With nearly three decades of hands-on experience in commercial landscaping, Matt brings a rare depth of expertise to every consultation. After owning and operating a successful commercial landscape business for 18 years in the Cincinnati, Ohio area, he's seen it all — from large-scale corporate campus maintenance to intricate seasonal planting rotations that keep properties looking sharp year-round.

Now, through Landscape Image Consulting, Matt is channeling that expertise into helping property managers, business owners, and homeowners across the Midwest make smarter decisions about their landscapes.

Whether you're dealing with a neglected commercial property that needs a turnaround plan, trying to choose the right plants for Ohio's unpredictable climate, or just want a professional set of eyes on your maintenance program — Matt's been there and done it.
